EMPLOYEE REFERRAL BONUS PROGRAM – UPDATED 9/9/22

Our Human Resources/Employment Department has updated our employee referral bonus program based on current open positions that have proven difficult to fill. We know our best referral source is from our own employees, and appreciate your help with our recruitment efforts.

HOW IT WORKS:

⇨ Refer a qualified candidate to a referral bonus-eligible open position (refer to chart, below). ⇨ With the exception of per diem positions, the candidate must not have been employed by the organization during     the past 12 months. ⇨ If the referred candidate is hired into the eligible position, the referring employee will receive the applicable bonus     payable in two installments ‐‐ 1st half on referred candidate’s date of hire; 2 nd half after referred candidate completes six     months of employment. Please note, Physicians and Dentists are not eligible to participate in this program. ⇨Be sure to ask the referred candidate to indicate your name on their application. ⇨ Questions about the Program may be directed to Recruitment@ehr.org.

MONTHLY CASH RAFFLE:

Additionally, each month the names of ALL employees who referred candidates who were hired into any position during the previous month are placed into a drawing for a $250 VISA gift card.  Winners are notified directly by the Human Resources/Employment team.
